# Why SMEs in Malta (and Abroad) Are Moving to Audit Marketplaces

For years, finding an auditor has been a headache for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). Endless phone calls. Back-and-forth emails. Waiting weeks for a single proposal.

But in 2025, things are changing. SMEs are discovering a faster, smarter way to connect with auditors: **digital marketplaces**.

From **Malta to international hubs**, more businesses are making the switch — and here’s why.

---

![smes-moving-to-audit-marketplaces.webp](/images/blog/smes-moving-to-audit-marketplaces.webp)

## The Old Way: Slow and Frustrating

Traditionally, SMEs had to:

- Ask friends or accountants for auditor recommendations.
- Call multiple firms just to get a quote.
- Send documents manually and chase responses.

Result? Lost time, hidden costs, and unnecessary stress.

---

## The New Way: Audit Marketplaces

Audit marketplaces flip the process. Instead of SMEs chasing auditors, auditors come to them.

✅ **How it works:**

- SMEs post their audit request once.
- Multiple licensed auditors send proposals.
- Businesses compare fees, timelines, and experience.
- Documents are shared securely in one platform.

It’s faster, fairer, and far more transparent.

---

## Why SMEs Are Making the Switch

1. **Speed** – Get multiple proposals in days, not weeks.
2. **Transparency** – Compare fees upfront, avoid hidden costs.
3. **Security** – Upload sensitive documents to one secure platform.
4. **Choice** – Access a wider pool of auditors, not just local contacts.
5. **Growth support** – Especially for startups, marketplaces help find auditors who understand scaling and investor needs.

---

## Malta’s Case: Why Local SMEs Benefit

In Malta, every limited liability company must undergo an annual statutory audit under the **Companies Act**. For SMEs, this often means scrambling to meet **MBR deadlines**.

Audit marketplaces like **VACEI** help Maltese SMEs by:

- Simplifying the search for qualified local auditors.
- Offering cost transparency (€2,000 – €15,000 range for SMEs).
- Avoiding last-minute deadline stress.
- Connecting startups and scale-ups with auditors who understand **GAPSME** rules.
