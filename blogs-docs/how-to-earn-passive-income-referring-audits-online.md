With platforms like **VACEI Platform**, you can earn money simply by introducing businesses to auditors. It’s a smart, low-effort way to generate **passive income** without needing to be an auditor yourself.

## Why Audit Referrals Work

Every business in Malta (and many abroad) needs an annual statutory audit. But finding the right auditor can be stressful, confusing, and time-consuming.

That’s where you come in:

- You refer a company to VACEI.
- They post their audit request.
- Once they complete their first audit through the platform, **you earn a commission**.

Simple, transparent, and scalable.

Your earnings depend on the size of the audits you refer. For example:

- Small business audit: referral fee might be **€100 – €200**.
- Medium company audit: referral fee could be **€300 – €500**.
- Larger audits: commissions scale up with value.

💡 The best part? Once you’ve referred a client, you keep earning every time they use the platform.

- 💼 **Entrepreneurs and freelancers** with networks of business owners.

If you know companies that need audits, you can earn.
