# VACEI Referral Program Explained: How You Get Paid for Referrals

Earning money from referrals sounds exciting, but how does it actually work? At **VACEI Platform**, we’ve designed our referral program to be **simple, transparent, and rewarding**.

Whether you’re a consultant, freelancer, or just someone with business contacts, you can earn cash every time you refer a company that completes their first audit through VACEI.

Here’s exactly how the program works — and how you get paid.

---

![sheetsway-referral-program.webp](/images/blog/sheetsway-referral-program.webp)

## Step 1: Sign Up for the Referral Program

- Join the **VACEI Referral Program** for free.
- Get your **unique referral code or link**.
- Share it directly with your contacts or post it online.

---

## Step 2: Refer Businesses That Need Audits

Every limited liability company in Malta — and many abroad — needs an annual statutory audit. That’s your opportunity.

- Send your referral link to SMEs, startups, or directors in your network.
- Encourage them to post their audit request on VACEI.
- Once they sign up using your link, they’re tracked as **your referral**.

---

## Step 3: The Client Completes Their Audit

- The referred business posts their audit request.
- They receive multiple proposals from licensed auditors.
- Once they choose an auditor and complete their first audit, the referral is confirmed.

---

## Step 4: You Get Paid

Here’s the best part:

- You earn a **commission** when your referral completes their first audit.
- Payments are **transparent and tracked** through the platform.
- The bigger the audit, the bigger your commission.

---

## Why Join the VACEI Referral Program?

- 🎯 **High-value commissions** – audits are worth thousands, so referral earnings add up quickly.
- 💼 **Professional credibility** – you’re helping businesses find trusted auditors.
- 🌍 **Scalable** – works for referrals in Malta and internationally.
- 🔒 **Transparent payouts** – no hidden rules, no surprises.

---

## Who Should Join?

The program is perfect for:

- Accountants or consultants who don’t perform audits themselves.
- Business advisors who know SMEs and startups.
- Entrepreneurs and freelancers with strong business networks.
- Students or professionals who want a **side hustle** with zero risk.

---

## Conclusion: Turn Connections Into Commissions

With the **VACEI Referral Program**, you don’t need to be an auditor to make money in the audit industry. Simply refer businesses, let them complete their audit through the platform, and collect your commission.

It’s transparent. It’s simple. And it works.
